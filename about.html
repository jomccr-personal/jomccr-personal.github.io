About Ouachita Labs

Bringing bleeding-edge AI solutions to Arkansas businesses with hometown values and global expertise.

The Story

From the Ouachitas to Seattle and Back

I've spent my entire life in Central Arkansas, growing up in the shadow of the Ouachita Mountains. These hills shaped my character - teaching me persistence, curiosity, and the value of community.

In 2019, I began my journey in software development, focusing on data engineering. A year at Amazon in Seattle gave me invaluable experience at scale, but Arkansas called me home. There's something special about this place - the natural beauty of Petit Jean, the healing waters of Hot Springs, and most importantly, the people.

Why Ouachita Labs?

After shipping multiple AI-powered MVPs and agents at Numerator - a leading market research technology company - I realized Arkansas businesses needed someone who understood both cutting-edge AI and local business needs.

Ouachita Labs was born from a simple belief: Arkansas companies deserve access to the same AI innovations as Silicon Valley, delivered by someone who understands their unique challenges and opportunities.
